# 📄 References & Papers

## Foundational Papers

| Paper | Authors | Year | Relevance |
|-------|---------|------|-----------|
| [Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space](https://arxiv.org/abs/1301.3781) | Mikolov et al. | 2013 | Word2Vec — the foundation of word embeddings |
| [GloVe: Global Vectors for Word Representation](https://nlp.stanford.edu/pubs/glove.pdf) | Pennington et al. | 2014 | GloVe embeddings |
| [BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.04805) | Devlin et al. | 2019 | Contextual embeddings |
| [Sentence-BERT: Sentence Embeddings using Siamese Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.10084) | Reimers & Gurevych | 2019 | Sentence-level embeddings |
